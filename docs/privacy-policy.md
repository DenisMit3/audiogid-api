# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 30, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Audiogid ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Data:** If you sign up, we may collect your phone number or Telegram ID for authentication.
- **Purchase Data:** Transaction history for in-app purchases. We do not store credit card details; these are handled by Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data:** We collect your geolocation to provide location-based tours and nearby content. You can opt-out via device settings. Background location is collected only if you explicitly enable "Tour Mode" background tracking.
- **Usage Data:** We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (screens visited, interactions) to improve the App.
- **Device Data:** Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (anonymous) for analytics and fraud prevention.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App's functionality (e.g., playing audio for nearby places).
- To process in-app purchases and verify receipts.
- To improve our services through analytics.
- To send push notifications (if enabled).

## 4. Disclosure of Your Information
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who perform services for us (e.g., Firebase for analytics/hosting, Vercel for backend).
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law.

## 5. Security
We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.

## 6. Retention
We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

## 7. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your account and associated data directly within the App settings or by contacting us.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@audiogid.app.
