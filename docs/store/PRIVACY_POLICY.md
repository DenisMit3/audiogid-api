# Privacy Policy for Audiogid

**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Audiogid ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy applies to the Audiogid mobile application (the "App") and explains how we collect, use, and share information about you.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us
*   **Account Information:** If you create an account, we may collect your email address, name, and password. *Note: The core features are available without an account.*
*   **Purchase Information:** If you make a purchase, the transaction is processed by the app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store). We receive a receipt validation token but do not store your credit card details.
*   **User Content:** Any lists, favorites, or reviews you create.

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
*   **Location Data:** To provide location-based audio guides and navigation (e.g., "Tour Mode"), we collect precise location data even when the app is in the background, if you grant us permission. This data is used solely to trigger audio narration and guide you along the route. It is not sold to third parties.
*   **Usage Data:** We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the app, such as which tours you listen to and how long you use the app.
*   **Device Information:** We collect information about your device, including model, OS version, and unique identifiers (for attribution and analytics).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
*   Process transactions and manage your entitlements.
*   Send you technical notices and support messages.
*   Monitor and analyze trends and usage.
*   Personalize your experience (e.g., showing nearby places).

## 4. Sharing of Information
We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
*   **Service Providers:** Who help us with hosting, analytics (e.g., Firebase), and support.
*   **Compliance:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 5. Data Safety (Google Play)
*   **Location:** Collected (App functionality), Optional (User can deny).
*   **Personal Info:** User IDs (if logged in).
*   **Financial Info:** Purchase history.
*   **Data Security:** All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
*   **Deletion:** Users can request account deletion via the App settings or our website.

## 6. Your Rights
You may update or delete your account information at any time through the App settings. You may also contact us to request data deletion.
